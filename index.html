Balea Lake

Bâlea Lake is located at an altitude of 2,000 meters, in one of the most picturesque places in Romania, in the Făgăraş Mountains, at a distance of 80 kilometers from the city of Sibiu. The road to get there, respectively the Transfăgărăşan road, also contributes to the beauty of the place. It is difficult to describe in words the beauty of the landscape you will have.

The Danube Delta

The Danube Delta is a unique place not only in Romania, but also in Europe, being considered "the youngest land" on the Old Continent. The moment you get here, you feel like you've entered another world, where time has stood still, and nature has taken over everything you see. The feeling is superb, and those who came here fell in love with the charm of the Delta.

Tourist attractions: Sacalin Island, Sulina Lighthouse, Danube Delta Ecotourism Museum Center, Murighiol Lake (Salt), Mile 23, Sfantu Gheorghe and many others.

It is the largest nature reserve in Europe.The Danube Delta is a region of great tourist beauty and of real scientific interest. The Danube Delta Biosphere Reserve ranks 5th among the Earth's wetlands and 2nd in Europe, but is 3rd in ecological importance in the world.

Danube boilers

Small Boilers and Large Boilers is the place where the Danube narrows the most in Romania, where the great Danube River flows through the narrowest and grandest area of the Danube Gorge.

The name of Boilers is given due to the vortices that form on the surface of the water due to the high flow that the Danube has in these rocky and narrow parts, where the turbulent water sometimes gives the impression that it is boiling.

Boiler region is near the city of Orșova, about 365 km from Bucharest. Coming from Drobeta Turnu-Severin, you pass by the largest dam in Romania, the Iron Gates and the border point with Serbia.

Bran Castle

The castle is built at the beginning of the entrance in the Rucar-Bran passage in the vicinity of DN 73 that connects Brasov with Campulung, only 30 km from Brasov and the distance from Bucharest to Bran castle is about 200 km.

Known in the past as the birthplace of the legend of Count Dracula, it still retains the truth of the myth and attracts hundreds of tourists each year.

Count Dracula is said to be a fictional character, and his name is inspired by the nickname given to Vlad Tepes, who was once considered a bloodthirsty and ruthless prince with humans.

However, it is true that Vlad Tepes is representative of Bran Castle, because he led a long time ago organizations that aimed to punish the German merchants present in the city of Brasov, who did not want to obey him.

Medieval city Sighisoara

It is the only inhabited fortified fortress in Romania and one of the few in Europe.

In the heart of the Sighisoara fortress, the small dream square The Citadel Square seems to be the perfect place among all these places to visit in Sighisoara to write an illustrated one.

Surrounded by several storeyed and stylish buildings, painted in beautiful pastel colors, it is one of the most beautiful medieval squares in Romania.

The Clock Tower offers an impressive reception at the entrance to the market.

One of the oldest buildings in the Citadel Square, the Deer House dates back to the 1300s and has deer antlers in one corner.
